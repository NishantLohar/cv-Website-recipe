Chole Bhature Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cup soaked overnight chickpeas
  • 2 chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup tomato puree
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon chana masala powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 tablespoon ginger
  • 3 tablespoon onion paste
  • salt as required
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 3 teaspoon yoghurt (curd)
  • refined oil as required
  • salt as required
  • 1/4 tablespoon wheat flour

Instruction

  1. Pressure cook the chickpeas
  2. Saute the onion
  3. Add spices and cook the chole
  4. Add some amchur powder to the chole
  5. Prepare the dough
  6. Cook the bhatura and serve
